1. What are the types of RL algorithms?
-Value-based, 
-policy-based, 
-actor-critic, 
-model-based, and 
-evolutionary and hybrid methods.
2. What are examples of value-based methods?
-SARSA, 
-Q-Learning, 
-Double DQN, and 
-Rainbow DQN
3. What are examples of policy-based methods?
-REINFORCE (Monte Carlo Policy Gradient), 
-Trust Region Policy Optimization (TRPO)
-Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO)
-Soft Actor-Critic (SAC)
4. What are examples of actor-critic methods?
-A3C (Asynchronous Advantage Actor-Critic)
-DDPG (Deep Deterministic Policy Gradient)
-TD3 (Twin Delayed DDPG)
-AlphaGo/AlphaZero
5. What are examples of model-based methods?
-Dyna-Q
-MuZero
6. What are examples of evolutionary/hybrid methods?
-Evolution Strategies (ES)
-NeuroEvolution
-Hybrid Algorithms
